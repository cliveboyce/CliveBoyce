Personal Notes:
1871 Census Housekeeper in household of the Lockyer family, and is sister in law to head. Teignmouth. There are 3 Lockyer children: Elizabeth or Bessie is 10, George 6 and Charles 4. (Ruth is the half sister of Patience Watts Brewer. Patience had married Levi Lockyer in 1857).

Ruth married George Pittwood Carlile in 1871.

George was a Ship Smith aged 52 in 31 March 1901 WestTeignmouth census. Ruth who was aged 51 was a grocer.

In 1911 Ruth's occupation again is General Shopkeeper Grocery.George's is a Blacksmith.

She appears in all England & Wales Census Records, 1881, 1891, 1901 and 1911 with her husband and no children recorded. They appear to have spent their lives in the Teignmouth District.

Ruth was the Beneficiary according to the Will of her husband. Probate records dated 24th May 1911 give George's death date as 20th April 1911.

England & Wales Death reg records a death for Ruth Carlile in 1921 aged 72.
