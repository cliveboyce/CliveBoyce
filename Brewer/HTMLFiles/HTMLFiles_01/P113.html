Marriage on 23 July 1801 at Heavitree, Devon (IGI). Tom’s data from the Heavitree records
(as copied by the vicar in 1934) give the banns on 38 (sic) June, 5 and 12 July 1801. Presumably
this should be 28 June, 5 and 12 July (all Sundays as expected). The curate was Peter Comys
Tucker and the witnesses were: Jacob Payne and Elizabeth Adams. Elizabeth is possibly Anna
Maria Adams’ sister and Jacob Payne her brother in law (he had married Anna Maria’s sister
Hagar in 1798). Also available ex FMP PR
