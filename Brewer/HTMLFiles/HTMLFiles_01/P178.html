Thomas married MARY LANGSFORD 04 February 1823 in Alphington daughter of JOHN and SARAH LANGSFORD.

Thomas Brewer, bachelor sojourner & Mary Brewer widow (in fact of Solomon William Brewer) were married 4th February 1823. The witnesses were Elizabeth Venn & Frances Turner (church clerk). Richard Venn was staying with John Brewer in 1851.
