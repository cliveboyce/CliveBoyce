Sarah’s father was William Brewer, limeburner and William Loram’s father was Thomas Loram, yeoman. William Loram was a bachelor living at Alphington. His occupation was butcher. Sarah was a spinster living at Fore Street Hill (presumably Exeter). Both were of full age ie both were born before 1820. The witnesses were Thomas and Mary Langsford.
