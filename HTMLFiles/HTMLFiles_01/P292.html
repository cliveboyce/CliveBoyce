Personal Notes:
1891 census Richard aged 24, had moved to 16 Market Hill, Sudbury. He was a furniture salesman. His wife Margaret was aged 25 born Marchwood, Hampshire.
1901 census was aged 34 (born in Exmouth) still living in Sudbury, Suffolk. He was an upholsterer, shopkeeper. Maggie (Margaret) his wife was aged 35. Their children (all born in Sudbury) were: Dorothy 8, Florence 7, Kate 3. Maggie's brother Jessie Abraham aged 17 was working for Richard. He was born Boldres, Lymington, Hampshire.
