1841 Henry Physick was aged 2, living at Rose Cottage, Alphington with his parents William, 35, gardener; Ann, 35; Ann, 5; and John 1? Month all born in county.
1851 Henry Physick was 11 and with his family in Alphington: William, head, 46, gardener, born Tavistock,; Ann, wife, 46, born Martin; Ann, 13, daughter; Ellen, 7, daughter; Charles William, 5, son; John,2, son; all children born Alphington.
1871 Henry Physick was living at Rose cottage, Alphington, aged 30 gardener of 12 acres employing several men and boys. Hs wife was Alice 29 born Alphington and their children were Ann L 6, Sarah J 5, William H 3, Annie M 2, Ellen M 5months. Henry’s parents: William Physick aged 67, a retired gardener, born Tavistock and his wife Ann, 65, born Tavistock were next in the enumeration (also living in Rose Cottage). Both Henry and William were given as Head of household.
1881 Henry Physick living at Hatherley House Alphington, Exeter, aged 41 born in Alphington. He was a nurseryman employing 4 men & 2 boys.
