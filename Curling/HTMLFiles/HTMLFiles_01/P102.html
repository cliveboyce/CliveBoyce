Personal Notes:
John Curling the elder of Hereson in St Lawrence, will dated 29 May 1573 proved 8 Jan 1573/4. PRC17/41/339 PRC16/61 C/2 [A41/339].
Margery was to inherit a house at Herson. He left tenements at Herson to his son Robert, also one at Dumpton, parish of SP, where Robert was living, his tenements at Norwood to his son Anthony where Anthony was living, and a tenement at Dumpton to his son William where William was living. He appointed his three sons executors.
John was overseer of the will of his brother Robert 1542, and was in remainder to property devised by Robert to his son Robert.
