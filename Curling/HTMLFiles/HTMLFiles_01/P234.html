WIll of Daniel Curling
TNA download PROB 11/2200 Image 112 (see also The Genalogist)

• Made 12 Jan 1850, proved 22 Nov 1854
• Gentleman of Chilton in parish of St Laurence, He owned one fifth part of Chilton farm in the occupation of Thomas Hooper and half of a property in Effingham Place, Ramsgate
• Thomas Hooper and heirs to hold this share for ever but payments to be made thereout to
• his daughter Ann Curling £200
• his son Daniel £200
• To his grandchildren Thomasin Bedford, Thomas Bedford, Mary Bedford, Gilbert Bedford and George Bedford £200 to be shared equally,
• To his daughter Ann Curling and her heirs his half part share in Effingham Place in the occupation of Thomas Chapman.
• to daughter Ann and her heirs the residue of his estate
• Thomas Hooper to be executor

Witnesses Francis Edwards junior of 17 Hart Street Bloomsbury and Horatio Groves of East Lane Bermondsey.


Francis Edwards junior was the son of Francis Edwards who married Eleanor Hooper see under Daniel Curling's daughter, Sarah Hope Curling’s will made 1847, proved 1857.
