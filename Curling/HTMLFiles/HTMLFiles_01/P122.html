Personal Notes:
Her mother's will establishes that she married Peter Harnett but only refers to one child: Mercy. Infact there were at least four others.
Her own will, ex the Genealogist, Mary, widow refers to her sons John and Peter, her daughter Mercy wife of James Stock and her late mother Mercy Curling, widow, witnesses Ann Hooper and John Fagg.
